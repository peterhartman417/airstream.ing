## 2026 Rally Schedule

### Arkansas Razorback Airstream Club Events

**February 19-22**: Unit Board of Trustees (UBT) - Stuttgart, AR. Join us for a weekend at Zach Bullock's magnificent estate in Stuttgart, the duck and rice capital of the world! There will be all sorts of fun and some sort of meeting. Emphasis on fun. Host: Zach Bullock.

**March 19-22**: Hope, Diamonds and Jonquil Rally - Hope Fairgrounds, Hope AR. Old Washington State Park Jonquil Festival, Diamond hunting at the Crater of Diamonds State Park, Tour of the Klipsch Speaker Museum and more! Hosts: Jesters and Belks.

**April 12+**: Spring Fling in Gulf Shores State Park. Make reservations at [Gulf State Park](https://reserve.alapark.com/gulf/campsites) (site #s 220-250). So many activities...Beach, Blue Angels, Seafood, Tiki Bar Cruises, Aviation Museum, live music, and so much more!

**May 7-10**: Bike Rack Brewing Co., Bentonville, Ark. Tentative - details to follow.

**May 14-17**: Bikes, Buddies, and Brewpubs at Downtown Riverside RV Park, N. Little Rock, AR. Morning bike rides on the Arkansas River trail and local bicycle pub crawls (e.g., Lost Forty, Stones Throw, Southern Tail Brewery, Camp Taco, Flyway). Make reservations through the Downtown Riverside RV Park at 501-340-5312 and request sites closest to river (mention Arkansas Airstream Club). Hosts: Paul & Jeannie Beck and Dana & Jason Landrum.

**August 22-27**: 69th Annual Airstream Club International Rally - Minot, ND. Fun, friendships, and all things Airstream.

**September 10-13**: Girls Just Wanna Have Fun. Location TBD. Hosts: Laura Wessinger & Terry Madden

**September 23-27**: Region 6 Rally - Unhitched RV, Foley, Ala.

**October 15-18**: 9th annual Airstreams on the Square in El Dorado, Ark. Plan for an out-of-this-world fun time at "Silver Trek: The Final Frontier" ... think Spock's pointy ears!

**October 22-25**: Arkaluma aka "Burning Mouse" - Museum of Automobiles, Petit Jean Mountain. Join your friends around the campfire and hear the tragic tale of two lovers with tails - Arka and Luma. This is a boondocking rally on beautiful Petit Jean Mountain near Morrilton, Ark.

**October 29 - November 1**: Halloween on the Rez! Jeff and Cindy have opened their place for a Halloween adventure in Jay, Okla. Costumes welcome!

**November 5-8**: Buffalo Point Rally, Buffalo National River. The end of the camping season would not be complete without the Buffalo Point Rally on America's first national river near Yellville, Ark. A hike to the Indian Rockhouse, and food, glorious food, are a few of the activities that delight Airstreamers each year.
